About Me

My name is Muhiddin and I love front-end development but I also get a kick out of anything design and web related. I enjoy figuring out how things work and making thoughtful, engaging, and functional web applications that have a clean and simple design. I've recently completed "100 days of code" challenge in 10 days by coding 10 hours a day.
